Spaghetti Bolognese

A classic Italian pasta dish made with rich tomato sauce, seasoned ground beef, and aromatic herbs. Perfect for a family dinner or a cozy night in!

Recipe Information

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty:Beginner

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and garlic, saut√© until softened and fragrant.
  3. Stir in the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add canned tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, and basil. Mix well.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then simmer for 30 minutes until thickened.
  6. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  7. Drain spaghetti, then combine with sauce or serve topped with sauce.
  8. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese before serving.

Tips

For a richer flavor, let the sauce simmer low and slow for an hour. You can also add chopped carrots and celery for a more authentic Italian touch.
